Name

Flappy Circle

Concept

This is a clone of the mobile game Flappy Bird, but with abstract shapes instead of birds. I would ideally like to have the obstacles more interesting than 2 pipes, but I'll see what I can do.

Genre

Arcade

Platform

Desktop only, not responsive

Story

Completely abstract

Aesthetics

Something similar to Geometry Dash? Maybe the player shape explodes upon colliding with an obstacle? Mainly just abstract shapes.

Gameplay

The player will press space to keep their sprite in the air just like Flappy Bird. They will have to avoid obstacles that are constantly moving towards them. At the bottom of the site, there will be a little tooltip saying "press space to jump".
